## Trumbo acreage discrepancy

Two credible sources describe the terminal reclamation differently:

- KWAHS: a **134-acre landfill**, inspected by Flagler in April 1911.
- NAVFAC: a **140-acre landfill area**, created in 1908 for tracks and warehouses.

These may reflect different dates, boundaries, rounding, or definitions of the developed site. The application displays both and does not silently harmonize them.

## NOAA vector-package acquisition status

The InPort records identify the projects and direct users to NOAA’s Shoreline Data Explorer. The current execution environment could not retrieve the binary download packages. No substitute linework has been created.
