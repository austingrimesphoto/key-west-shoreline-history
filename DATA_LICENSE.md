# Data and media rights

The MIT license applies to original source code in this repository. It does not automatically apply to historical data, maps, scans, photographs, or derived geospatial products.

## General rules

- Preserve the institution, title, identifier, permanent URL, date, and rights statement for every source item.
- Do not republish full-resolution archival imagery unless the item’s terms allow it.
- Prefer links, IIIF services, or institution-hosted thumbnails where permitted.
- NOAA datasets should be credited to the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration and retain their metadata.
- Library of Congress Sanborn material identified by the Library as public domain may be reused with a credit line.
- University of Florida, Florida Memory, Florida Keys History Center, and KWAHS items require item-specific review.
- Derived geometry must retain source provenance and must not imply greater accuracy than the source supports.

The source ledger is the controlling record for item-level use.
